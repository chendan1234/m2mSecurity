Please check the problem as follows:

First, Check your Mobile Phone Network Signal

If your network signal is bad, this may cause network communication problems, our App cannot load the necessary information.
You can find a place with good signal, or choose other good signal network.

Second, Check If the Network Permission for the App is Open

If you shut down the network permission we nedd, we won't be able to load any data even if your phone has good signal.
You can enter the "Settings - Cellular", select our application and check if the network permissions are open.

Third, Check the Mobile Phone System and Model

If your mobile phone is a Chinese version, and your system is iOS 10, then you may encounter a problem caused by iOS. You need to:
1. enter the "Settings - Cellular" and turn on the "Wi-Fi Assistant"
2. in the "Cellular", switch on and off the permission of any App
3. close our application process and restart iPhone

This should solve your problem
